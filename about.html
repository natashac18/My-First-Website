na_ART_chi: A name worth a thousand conversations…



I have always had a knack and a passion for arts and crafts, being able to design, draw, paint and create masterpieces out of scrap. During my teenage years, I attended an art school called Jack Meyer Kunsentrum where I specialized in and enhanced my God-given talent in visual arts and design textiles. So for me, art is a way of life, a means to express myself.
In 2020, I established the brand na_ART_chi. The name is cleverly derived from the first syllables of my name and surname, with the word “ART” incorporated into it to sound the South African word for tangerine: “naartjie”. The aim is to showcase not only my talent, but that of all creatives out there, and put it up for sale. So, let me take you on a magical journey through the world of art, a world of inexplicable beauty and meaning.

Natasha Chitongo
Founder of na_ART_chi
